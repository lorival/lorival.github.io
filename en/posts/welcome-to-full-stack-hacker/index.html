Welcome to Full Stack Hacker

Hey hacker, I hope you’re doing great! I would like to welcome you to this small space where I will share a little of my experiences and interests in various areas related to software development. welcome to the Full Stack Hacker.

I am Lorival, I live in Portugal and I work with software development since 2005, I worked mainly with systems development for WEB, but for 3 years I also developed apps for mobile devices, both Android and IOS. I’ve owned a company, I worked as an independent consultant and freelancer, I did a little bit of everything and I made many, many, many mistakes that brought me here :) I currently work on a security team at Critical Techworks, a joint venture between BMW Group and Critical Software, with a focus on building software for the cars of the future.

I created this space to share a little bit about what I’ve learned on this journey so far and also what I’m learning today, my goal is to give back to the community everything I’ve learned from it, for free of course, for this I created a channel on youtube called Full Stack Hacker.

I am super excited about this new project, impossible to know what the future holds, but I hope that somehow I can contribute a little and bring to you some relevant, important and interesting content.

Ah! I’m a bit of a stutterer, I’ve been working on this my whole life, and I’m not familiar with cameras or public presentations, so I think this project will help me improve my communication while reinforcing what I’ve already learned. This way everyone wins :)

I haven’t started posting yet, as I’m preparing material, guidelines, playlists and designing formats. I thought about just turning on the camera and recording, but this is also an opportunity to explore creativity, so I want to do something that is fun for me too. I do not pretend to be youtuber, that is, live on youtube. I have my job and I have no interest in leaving, this project will be an extra activity for sharing, discussion and learning.

The videos will start in January 2020 part 2, a.k.a. 2021, and I will start with a basic and relevant topic for all of us, cyber security for IT professionals.

The videos will be published on the channel in Portuguese, so you will need subtitles, because my focus will be Portuguese speakers. I will have a post here on this blog with my notes and relevant information. To be notified to a new video subscribe there (no link yet, I know, but in the first video I put the link here);)

Best
